Image Segmentation

Divide an image into regions or categories which correspond to different objects or parts of objects [1]

About image segmentation

Image segmentation partitions an image into a set of regions or classes. It is a preprocessing step for a variety computer vision tasks, and it is used in face and object detection as well as medical image analysis.

There are a variety of techniques depending on the task. Image segmentation is a challenging problem because each image will have different characteristics, and so it is difficult to predict which ones to leverage. For instance, images have a variety of intensities and textures.
